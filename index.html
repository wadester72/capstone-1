Bacon Burger Recipe dinner for (2)

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • slices of bacon
  • Eggs, large Optional
  • hamburger buns
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: lettuce, tomato, onion, cheese, pickles, or even add an egg
    condiments (ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise or our in house special sauce.)

Baking Instructions

  1. Cook the bacon in a skillet over medium heat until crispy. Remove the bacon from
    the skillet and drain on paper towels. Set aside.
  2. Divide the ground beef into 2 equal portions and shape each portion into a patty.
    Season both sides of the patties with salt and pepper.
  3. In the same skillet used to cook the bacon, cook the burger patties over medium-high heat
    for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until they reach your desired level of doneness.
  4. While the burgers are cooking, toast the hamburger buns either in the skillet or in a toaster.
  5. Once the burgers are cooked, assemble the burgers by placing each patty on a toasted bun.
    Add 2 slices of bacon on top of each patty, and then add your desired toppings.
  6. Serve the bacon burgers hot and enjoy!

    Feel free to customize your bacon burgers with your favorite toppings and condiments.
    Enjoy your delicious homemade bacon burgers!
